Privacy Policy for SmartKitch
Effective Date: March 4, 2026
SmartKitch respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the SmartKitch application collects, uses, and protects user information.
SmartKitch is an AI-powered smart kitchen assistant designed to help users manage kitchen inventory, reduce food waste, discover recipes, and organise grocery shopping.

1. Information We Collect
SmartKitch may collect limited information necessary to operate the application.
Account Information
When users register or log in, the app may collect:
* Email address
* Authentication information via Firebase Authentication
* Basic profile information such as name and preferences
This information is used solely for user authentication and personalization of the application.
User-Generated Data
Users may voluntarily provide:
* Food inventory items
* Expiry dates
* Shopping lists
* Saved recipes
* Dietary preferences
This data is used only to provide core functionality of the app.
Camera Access
SmartKitch requests camera permission to allow users to scan food items or ingredients for quick inventory entry.
Camera data is not stored, recorded, or shared, and is used only for the intended feature within the application.
Device Information
Basic device-related information may be collected automatically such as:
* Device type
* Operating system version
* App performance logs
This helps improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use the Information
The collected information is used to:
* Authenticate users securely
* Manage kitchen inventory
* Provide AI-based recipe recommendations
* Generate grocery shopping lists
* Send expiry reminders and notifications
* Improve the functionality and performance of the application
We do not sell or rent user data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security
SmartKitch uses Firebase services provided by Google to securely store and manage user data.
Security measures include:
* Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
* Secure authentication systems
* Restricted database access
While we strive to protect user information, no system can guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing
SmartKitch does not share personal data with third parties except when required to operate essential services such as Firebase.
Third-party services used:
* Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore Database, Storage)
These services follow their own privacy and security standards.

5. User Control and Data Rights
Users have full control over their information and may:
* Edit profile information
* Modify or delete inventory items
* Delete saved recipes or shopping lists
* Request account deletion
Users can uninstall the application at any time to stop data collection.

6. Children's Privacy
SmartKitch is not intended for children under the age of 13.We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
If we become aware that such information has been collected, it will be removed promptly.

7. Permissions Used by the App
SmartKitch may request the following permissions:
Camera Permission
* Used only to scan food items and ingredients
* No images are stored or shared
Internet Access
* Required for Firebase services and AI-powered recipe suggestions

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
